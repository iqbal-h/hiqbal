I am a fourth year PhD student in WISN Lab at (North Carolina State University) CS department, advised by Prof. Muhammad Shahzad. Before joining NC State, I received an MSc in Electrical Engineering (Networks) from LUMS in 2018. In 2012, I received a BSc in Telecommunication Engineering from NUCES, Lahore.

Research Interests

My research interest spans networking and performance evaluation, systems, and machine learning. More precisely, I am currently working on two thrusts: 1) quality of service and quality of experience analysis of video and game streaming applications and performance characterization of the global network infrastructure of a public cloud, and 2) design of application and transport layer mechanisms, augmented by deep learning, for improving quality of experience of streaming applications in the cloud.
