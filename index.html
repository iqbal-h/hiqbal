I am a second year PhD student in WISN Lab at (North Carolina State University) CS department, advised by Prof. Muhammad Shahzad. My area of research is cloud computing and data center networks.

Before joining NC State, I received an MSc in Electrical Engineering (Networks) from LUMS in 2018. In 2012, I received a BSc in Telecommunication Engineering from NUCES, Lahore.

Research Interests

My research interest spans cloud computing, internet measurement, machine learning, and wireless networking. I am currently working on use of deep learning for performance enhancement of cloud applications. Previously, I have conducted a large scale study on cloud network characterization. I have also worked on wireless heterogeneity of IoT devices.
